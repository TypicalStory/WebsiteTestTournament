Welcome to LifeLine

LifeLine is a seasonal Minecraft competition: €4 entrance, €100 prize. Our mission is to offer a fun, competitive atmosphere and great entertainment for viewers.

Events

Snowy Rides — Winter

Entrance: €4 · Prize: €100
Ice-biome arenas, low visibility, and slippery terrain. Strategy and positioning win the day.
More details
  • Format: Solo, double-elimination
  • Rules: No hacks/macros; vanilla balance
  • Tip: Plan for resource scarcity

Dead in the Hot — Summer

Entrance: €4 · Prize: €100
Desert maps with heat-haze and long sightlines. Resource control and ranged aim matter most.
More details
  • Format: Duos, best-of-three
  • Rules: Shared inventory caps; no cross-team comms
  • Bonus: Mid-match supply drops

Falling Leaves — Autumn

Entrance: €4 · Prize: €100
